• Sleep App is intended to create isochronic tone cycles mimicking the sleep cycle to enhance sleep quality and to wakeup easy and fresh.
  • The developers of Sleep App do not endorse any claim relevance to the theory or practice of brainwave entrainment.
  • The tones are modulated Brownian noise representing different brain wave frequencies corresponding to different stages of sleep.
  • Sleep App doesnt require you to wear head phones. Just start the brain wave and set volume between low to medium volume which you feel comfortable and sleep normally.
  • To completely exit, choose press exit button in the options menu..
